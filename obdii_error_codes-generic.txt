20211230
Viktor Godard

List from https://www.evilution.co.uk/mod/obdii-error-codes.htm

Below is a list of generic trouble codes that can be read by a fault code reader or an X-Gauge. Each car manufacturer has specific codes for their vehicles including the smart. As yet, no smart specific codes have been added to this list.
